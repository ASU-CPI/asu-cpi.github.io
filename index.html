Welcome to the Center for Policy Informatics.

The Center for Policy Informatics is a translational research hub at Arizona State University that advances the study, teaching, and practice of the nebulous field of policy informatics to address complex public policy and administration problems. Learn more.

Categories


The Center engages in a variety of interdisciplinary and cross-sector research activities, including:


  • Societal Change


  • Policy Analysis


  • Design Interventions


  • Field Building

Context


The interdisciplinary and cross-sector research activities of the Center transcends multiple context areas, including:


  • Health


  • Education


  • Environment


  • Governance

Network


The Center for Policy Informatics is comprised of an interdisciplinary, collaborative, and continually expanding network of people and organizations. The network is connected by their shared interest in advancing the research and practice of understanding and facilitating efficient, effective,and legitimate policy making and implementation in an increasingly complex and interconnected world. We invite you to explore and join the network.
